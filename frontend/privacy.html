Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 21, 2026

1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how Rusman ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use this website.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: email address, password hash, role, optional username, and optional avatar URL.
  • Profile and comments: profile details you provide and comments you publish (including timestamp and associated user ID).
  • Authentication data: secure authentication cookie used to keep you signed in.
  • Staff content uploads: image files submitted by authorized staff for post media handling.
  • Summary feature input: when summary generation is requested, article text required for summary generation may be processed by configured AI providers.

3. How we use your data

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Authenticate sessions and protect account access.
  • Provide commenting and profile functionality.
  • Generate article summaries when a user explicitly requests summary generation.
  • Moderate content and prevent misuse.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve website reliability and security.

4. Legal bases under GDPR

  • Contract: to provide account features you request.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure and improve our services.
  • Legal obligation: where processing is required by applicable law.
  • Consent: where specifically requested (if applicable in future features).

5. Cookies and similar technologies

We use an authentication cookie to keep users signed in. This cookie is configured with security controls such as HttpOnly and SameSite. We do not currently run third-party advertising trackers on this website.

6. Data sharing and processors

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers strictly for operating the website, such as cloud media hosting for uploaded images and AI model providers for requested summary generation. These providers process data under contractual safeguards.

7. AI summary processing

When you click the summary button on a post page, relevant article text may be sent to the configured AI provider (for example Groq or OpenAI) to generate a concise summary. This feature is optional and user-triggered.

  • We request summary output in Greek under current configuration.
  • If AI is unavailable, the system may return a local fallback summary.
  • Summary generation may be rate-limited for abuse prevention.

8. International transfers

Where service providers process data outside your country or the EEA, we apply appropriate safeguards (such as contractual protections) as required by applicable law.

9. Data retention

We keep account and related content data for as long as your account remains active and as needed for service operation, security, dispute handling, and legal compliance. You may request deletion, subject to legal or security retention needs.

10. Security measures

  • Passwords are stored as hashes, not plain text.
  • Authentication uses signed tokens and secure cookie settings.
  • Access controls are applied for staff-only functionality.
  • Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect data.

11. Your GDPR rights

Where GDPR applies, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object to certain processing, and request data portability. You may also lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

12. Children’s privacy

This website is not intended for children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16.

13. Contact and requests

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at info@rusman.gr.

14. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
