Cookie Policy

Effective date: February 26, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser to help websites work properly, remember settings, and measure performance.

2. What we collect

  • Essential cookies: required for core functionality such as authentication and keeping you signed in.
  • Analytics preference: your consent choice for analytics tracking (stored in your browser local storage).
  • Ads preference: your consent choice for ad personalization (stored in your browser local storage).
  • Consent timestamp: when your cookie choices were saved.

3. Cookie categories

  • Essential (always on): needed for secure login, session handling, and basic site operation.
  • Analytics (optional): helps us measure usage and improve page performance.
  • Ads (optional): used for ad-related behavior when enabled.

4. How we store your preferences

Your cookie preferences are saved in your browser under a local storage key and can be changed anytime from the "Cookie settings" control (footer) or the cookie preference modal.

5. How to change or withdraw consent

You can update your choices at any time via the cookie preferences popup and this policy is linked directly from that flow. You can also clear browser cookies/local storage from your browser settings.

6. Third-party services

Some features (such as ads or analytics) may rely on third-party services when enabled. Their technologies are subject to their own privacy/cookie policies.

7. Contact

For cookie or privacy questions, contact us at info@rusman.gr.
